Bio

Urban Planning Master's Candidate. Reader of comics, programming and electronics hobbiest, and user of data processing tools. Perhaps, these interests will combine to make an acceptable city. I'm Mexican-American, grew up in South Central, Los Angeles, and got my first computer when I was 13. I hacked my first Playstation Portable at 15 and later programmed my first homebrew application and theme for the system. I attended Universty of California, Santa Cruz, where I recieved a B.A. in History of Art and Visual Culture.
